Welcome to LearnEase

This is a fantastic place for Dyslexic students to start learning!

Welcome to LearnEase, a platform designed with dyslexia students in mind. We understand the unique challenges faced by dyslexic learners and have crafted a comprehensive learning experience tailored to their needs.

Our mission is to empower dyslexic students by providing practical, hands-on learning opportunities that promote understanding, confidence, and success. Through innovative features and interactive exercises, we strive to make learning accessible and enjoyable for every individual.

Key Features:

Text-to-Speech Feature:

Our text-to-speech feature enables students to listen to text, aiding comprehension and pronunciation. This tool helps students grasp concepts more effectively and develop their language skills.

Speech-to-Text Feature:

With our speech-to-text feature, students can practice forming sentences and understanding sentence structure. This feature promotes language production and reinforces grammar and syntax rules.

Quiz Page:

Our quiz page offers thought-provoking questions to stimulate critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Engaging with these quizzes encourages active learning and enhances cognitive abilities.

Listen and Answer Feature:

Our listen and answer feature challenges students to improve their listening skills by listening to audio prompts and responding in writing. This exercise strengthens auditory processing and reinforces comprehension skills.
